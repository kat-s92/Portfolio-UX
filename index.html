    About Me üë©üèª

    Over the last eight months, I have been working on completing my UX diploma and building up my skills to take the leap towards a career in user experience design. I wanted to learn to create something, something that focused on people's needs and something that they enjoyed using.

    Prior to that, I worked on some front-end development projects and that is what brought me to exploring UX further. I really like the psychological aspect of UX design and hope to combine that with my technical skills in my work.

    I have close to eight years of professional experience from the fields of Client Support, Human Resources and ESL teaching. As a teacher, I used many design principles from research to user testing to craft the most engaging, learner-centered lessons for my audience. While designing a lesson, you're ultimately working towards the goal of assessing the student and just like in UX design, you can either make the journey smooth and pleasant or do the bare minimum as long as you get the student to the end. I of course wanted my students to enjoy the interaction with their teacher and learning materials.

    Work aside - I love learning, being out in the nature, discovering new places and chocolate! That's just a bit about me, I'd love to hear from you if you liked my portfolio! üòä
